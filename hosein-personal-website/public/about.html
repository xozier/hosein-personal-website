About Me

Theoretical Physicist | PhD Candidate | Lifelong Learner

Academic Journey

I am Hosein Gholami, currently pursuing a PhD in Physics at the Technical University of Darmstadt in Germany. My research focuses on understanding the complexities of quark matter, particularly in the context of neutron star mergers. By utilizing effective models like the Nambu-Jona-Lasinio (NJL) model and incorporating methods such as Dyson-Schwinger Equations (DSE), I aim to explore and contribute to our understanding of the universe's most extreme conditions.

My academic path includes:

  • B.Sc. in Physics - Shiraz University
  • M.Sc. - Sharif University of Technology
  • Research collaborations with Goethe University and Giessen University

Beyond Academia

But there's more to me than just theoretical models and research papers. I have a deep curiosity about the world, which extends beyond academic study. I'm passionate about:

  • Languages - Exploring different cultures and societies
  • Music - Finding solace in playing the guitar
  • Gaming - Exploring virtual worlds through PC games

This blend of academic rigor and personal exploration shapes who I am, both as a researcher and as an individual. Through this website, I invite you to explore my professional work and personal passions. Whether you're here to learn about my research or simply share an interest in any of my hobbies, I hope you find something that resonates with you. Thank you for visiting!
